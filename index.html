About Us

More Than Just
Archery

Archery Lapangan Banteng was founded with a simple vision: to create a space where archers of all levels can grow, connect, and compete. Located in the heart of Jakarta, our range offers state-of-the-art facilities and a passionate community.

From beginners picking up a bow for the first time to national-level athletes honing their craft, we believe archery is a journey of discipline, focus, and joy.
